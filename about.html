About Us

Canara HRD Consultants is a Professional Management Service provider focusing on RECRUITMENT SERVICES, HUMAN RESOURCE SERVICES, Manpower Outsourcing and Corporate Training in the field of Manufacturing, IT and Service industries. We have been offering our services to Automobile, Manufacturing & Engineering giants, Electronics (EMS), Research & Development, Garment & Apparel, Pharmaceutical, Insurance Companies, FMCG, Civil & Constructions etc. Our Capability of searching from CEO to Front line levels is our greatest strength.
